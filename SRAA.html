Surah Al-Fatiha

بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ
In the name of Allah, the Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful.
This is the first verse of the Quran and is recited before every surah except Surah At-Tawbah. It emphasizes Allah's attributes of mercy and compassion.

Daily Duas & Adhkar

Morning Adhkar

أَصْبَحْنَا وَأَصْبَحَ الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ

"We have reached the morning and at this very time all sovereignty belongs to Allah. Praise is to Allah. There is none worthy of worship but Allah, alone, without partner."

Benefits

Reciting this dua in the morning brings protection from harm and increases one's provision for the day. The Prophet (ﷺ) would recite this every morning.

Evening Adhkar

أَمْسَيْنَا وَأَمْسَى الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ، لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لَا شَرِيكَ لَهُ

"We have reached the evening and at this very time all sovereignty belongs to Allah. Praise is to Allah. There is none worthy of worship but Allah, alone, without partner."

Benefits

Reciting this dua in the evening brings protection from harm during the night and ensures Allah's care and mercy throughout the night.

Sleeping Duas

بِاسْمِكَ رَبِّـي وَضَعْـتُ جَنْبِـي ، وَبِكَ أَرْفَعُـه، فَإِنْ أَمْسَـكْتَ نَفْسِـي فَارْحَـمْها ، وَإِنْ أَرْسَلْتَـها فَاحْفَظْـها بِمـا تَحْفَـظُ بِـه عِبـادَكَ الصّـالِحِـين

"In Your name my Lord, I lie down and in Your name I rise, so if You should take my soul then have mercy upon it, and if You should return my soul then protect it in the manner You do so with Your righteous servants."

Eating Duas

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ وَعَلَى بَرَكَةِ اللَّهِ

"In the name of Allah and with the blessings of Allah I begin (eating)."

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَطْعَمَنَا وَسَقَانَا وَجَعَلَنَا مُسْلِمِينَ

"All praise is due to Allah who has fed us and given us drink and made us Muslims."

Travel Duas

سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي سَخَّرَ لَنَا هَذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لَهُ مُقْرِنِينَ وَإِنَّا إِلَى رَبِّنَا لَمُنْقَلِبُونَ

"Glory to Him who has brought this under our control, though we were unable to control it. Indeed, to our Lord we will return."
